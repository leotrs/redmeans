about the project

each photograph captures a subject, partially or completely red, isolated in a monochrome world.

the literal (a tomato, a stop sign) becomes abstract (patience, decorative). red means what we decide it means.

inviting the question: what does red mean here?

process

shot on android. edited on android. black and white, sometimes sepia. but the red remains.
